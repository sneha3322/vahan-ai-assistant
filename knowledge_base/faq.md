# Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is my data sent to the cloud?**  
A: Never. All processing occurs locally on your device.

**Q: Can I use this offline?**  
A: Yes! Internet is only required for initial download and updates.

**Q: How do I upgrade my plan?**  
A: Go to Settings > Subscription and select "Upgrade"

**Q: What languages are supported?**  
A: Currently English, with Spanish and French in beta.
